<p><p>Commercial hot food holding cabinets can be classified into two types: visible and invisible markets. In visible market types, the cabinets are designed with transparent doors or windows that allow customers to easily view the food items inside. This enables customers to make informed choices and encourages impulse purchases. On the other hand, invisible market types have solid doors or panels, concealing the food items from the customers' view. These cabinets are commonly used in self-service areas or backstage kitchens, where the focus is on maintaining the food's temperature and quality rather than visually tempting customers.</p></p>
