<p><p>The liquid filter cartridges market comprises three main types: membrane filter cartridges, pleated filter cartridges, and depth filter cartridges. Membrane filter cartridges contain a thin, porous membrane that helps trap and remove small particles from liquid. Pleated filter cartridges have pleated filter media, offering a larger surface area for filtration and higher dirt-holding capacity. Depth filter cartridges use a thick, fibrous matrix to capture particles through depth filtration. These cartridges are crucial in industries where liquid filtration is essential, such as pharmaceutical, food and beverage, and water treatment.</p></p>
