<p><p>Enterprise Single Sign-On (SSO) is a technology that allows users to access multiple applications and systems within an organization using one set of login credentials. There are two main market types for SSO: on-premise and cloud. On-premise SSO is implemented within an organization's infrastructure, ensuring that access control and authentication processes are maintained internally. In contrast, cloud SSO is provided through a cloud-based service, where a third-party manages the SSO solution, offering accessibility and scalability advantages. Each market type caters to different organizational needs and preferences in terms of security, control, and infrastructure management.</p></p>
