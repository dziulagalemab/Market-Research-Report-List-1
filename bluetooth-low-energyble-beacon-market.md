<p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon market consists of various types, including iBeacon, Eddystone, and others. iBeacon is a beacon format developed by Apple, primarily used in iOS devices. It transmits unique identifiers to nearby smartphones, enabling location-based interactions. Eddystone, created by Google, is a more versatile beacon format compatible with both iOS and Android devices. It provides various frame types, including URL, UID, and TLM, offering greater flexibility for different applications. The "Others" category encompasses alternative formats and technologies that may have different features or be developed by different companies.</p></p>
