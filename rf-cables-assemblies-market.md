<p><p>The RF cable assemblies market consists of three main types: Semi-Rigid RF Cable Assemblies, Semi-Flexible RF Cable Assemblies, and Flexible RF Cable Assemblies. Semi-Rigid RF Cable Assemblies are made of solid inner conductors, offering high performance and stability but limited flexibility. Semi-Flexible RF Cable Assemblies provide moderate flexibility due to their partially stranded inner conductors. Flexible RF Cable Assemblies offer the highest level of flexibility, as they are made of stranded inner conductors. These different types cater to varying needs in industries like telecommunications, military, aerospace, and medical.</p></p>
