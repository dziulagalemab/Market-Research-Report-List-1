<p><p>Hospital sewage treatment equipment market types include pre-treatment, primary treatment, secondary treatment, tertiary treatment, and other market segments. Pre-treatment involves the removal of solids, oils, and grease from the sewage before further treatment. Primary treatment includes physical processes like sedimentation to remove suspended solids. Secondary treatment employs biological processes to remove dissolved organic matter and nutrients. Tertiary treatment utilizes advanced techniques such as filtration and disinfection to fully purify the wastewater. The "other" market segment encompasses supplementary equipment or technologies used in sewage treatment facilities that may not fall under the aforementioned types.</p></p>
