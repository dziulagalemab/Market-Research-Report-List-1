<p><p>Ceramic pressure casting machines are used in the manufacturing of ceramic products, and they are available in two market types: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They can perform all the necessary tasks, such as mold filling, pressure application, and mold release, without manual assistance. This makes them highly efficient and suitable for large-scale production.</p><p>Semi-automatic machines, on the other hand, require some manual intervention. While they can still carry out key processes automatically, certain tasks, like mold filling or mold release, may need operator assistance. These machines are often used in smaller production facilities or for custom-made products where flexibility is required.</p></p>
