Ferrite absorbing coatings utilize magnetic materials to convert electromagnetic waves into heat energy. Nano wave absorbing coatings use nanostructured materials to absorb and dissipate electromagnetic waves. Conductive polymer absorbing coatings use conductive polymers to absorb and disperse electromagnetic waves. Radioisotope absorbing coatings incorporate radioactive isotopes to absorb radiation.
