One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for flame retardant materials in various industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics. Aluminum hydroxide nanoparticles are extensively used as flame retardants due to their ability to release water vapor when exposed to heat, thereby suppressing combustion and reducing fire risks.</p><p>Additionally, the growing emphasis on environmental and safety regulations is also boosting the market. Aluminum hydroxide nanoparticles are considered environmentally friendly flame retardants as they release non-toxic gases during combustion, unlike traditional halogen-based flame retardants.</p><p>Furthermore, the expanding applications of aluminum hydroxide nanoparticles in industries such as pharmaceuticals, paints and coatings, and cosmetics are contributing to market growth.
